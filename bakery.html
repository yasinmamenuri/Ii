Our Story

Founded in 2010, Crumb & Craft began as a small family-owned bakery with a passion for traditional baking techniques. What started as a humble neighborhood shop has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our commitment to quality and craftsmanship.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, working closely with farmers and producers who share our values. Our bakers combine time-honored methods with innovative approaches to create products that are both familiar and exciting.

Every item in our bakery is made from scratch daily, ensuring freshness and flavor in every bite. From our signature sourdough to our decadent pastries, we pour our hearts into everything we make.
